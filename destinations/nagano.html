Discover Nagano

Nagano, host of the 1998 Winter Olympics, is a mountain city famous for Zenko-ji Temple, one of Japan's most important Buddhist temples. The surrounding area offers world-class skiing, the famous snow monkeys bathing in hot springs at Jigokudani, and scenic hiking trails. The region produces soba noodles and sake, and provides access to traditional post towns along the historic Nakasendo trail.
