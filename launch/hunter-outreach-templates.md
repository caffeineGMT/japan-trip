## Hunter Relationship Building (Before Ask)

### Initial Engagement (Week 1-2)

**Twitter Interactions:**
- Like their tweets daily
- Retweet with thoughtful comments
- Reply to their questions
- Share their hunts

**Example Comment:**
```
Just tried [product they hunted] - the UX is incredible. The [specific feature]
is exactly what I needed. Great hunt! 🔥
```

---

### Value-First Approach (Week 3-4)

**Offer Help:**
```
Hey [Name], saw your tweet about [problem]. I built something similar for
[your product]. Happy to share the code/approach if it helps. No strings attached!
```

**Share Their Content:**
```

```

```
curious about your take on the market.
```

## What NOT to Do

❌ **Don't:**
- Mass DM 50 hunters with the same template
- Ask for a hunt in the first message
- Offer money (against PH guidelines)
- Be pushy or desperate
- Send LinkedIn cold messages (use Twitter)
- Copy-paste without personalization

✅ **Do:**
- Personalize every message
- Build a relationship first
- Offer value before asking
- Be genuine and authentic
- Respect their time
- Follow up once (max twice)

---

## Success Metrics for Hunter Outreach

**Week 1-2:**
- 10 DMs sent (personalized)
- 3-4 responses expected
- 1-2 conversations started

**Week 3-4:**
- 2-3 product demos given
- 1 hunter interested (50% commit)
- Backup plan if no hunter (self-hunt)

**Week 5-6:**
- 1 hunter confirmed
- Launch details finalized
- Assets shared

**Launch Day:**
- Hunter posts at 12:01 AM PT
- Founder first comment within 1 minute
- Coordinated social media push

## Alternative: Self-Hunt Strategy

If you can't get a hunter (totally fine!):

**Advantages:**
- Full control over timing and copy
- No coordination needed
- Can edit on the fly
- Authentic founder story

**How to Succeed:**
1. Build PH karma (comment on other products for 2 months)
2. Create amazing product (most important)
3. Rally your network (friends, family, email list)
4. Be active all day (respond to everything)
5. Cross-promote aggressively (Twitter, Reddit, HN)

**Example Self-Hunt Intro:**
```
Hey Product Hunt! 👋 I'm self-hunting my first product.

Why? Because I couldn't find a hunter and I'm tired of waiting. Sometimes you
just have to launch.

I built this after getting lost in Kyoto with a dead phone. If you're planning
a Japan trip, this might save you some stress.

Would love your feedback - both positive and critical. I'm here all day.

Let's do this! 🚀
```

---

## Final Tips

1. **Start early** - 2 months before launch minimum
2. **Be genuine** - Hunters can smell desperation
3. **Offer value** - Give before you ask
4. **Build relationships** - Not just transactional
5. **Have a backup plan** - Self-hunt is perfectly fine
6. **Don't give up** - Most successful launches are self-hunted

**Remember:** The product quality matters more than who hunts it. A great product
will rise regardless of the hunter.

Good luck! 🚀
