I spent countless hours planning my Kyoto trip last year - juggling Google Maps,

train routes. I got lost, missed peak bloom, and wished I had a better tool.

your itinerary constantly.

## Risk Mitigation

**What if we don't get a hunter?**
- Self-hunt (perfectly acceptable)
- Focus on community upvoting
- Lean harder on personal network

**What if we launch on a busy day?**
- Have backup date ready (April 2 or 3)
- Monitor PH calendar for major launches
- Avoid launching same day as YC Demo Day

**What if the site goes down?**
- Pre-test with load testing tools
- Have Vercel auto-scaling enabled
- Monitor uptime with UptimeRobot
- Prepare "We're experiencing high traffic" message

**What if we get negative comments?**
- Respond professionally and quickly
- Acknowledge issues, commit to fixes
- Don't get defensive
- Use as learning opportunity
