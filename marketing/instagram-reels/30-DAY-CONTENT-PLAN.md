# Instagram Reels: "Hidden Japan Spots" Series - 30-Day Content Plan

## Campaign Overview

**Target:** #japantravel (14M posts)
**Duration:** 30 days (1 Reel/day)
**Format:** 15-30 seconds max, hook in first 3 seconds
**Goal:** 50K Reel views → 1,000 profile visits → 200 link clicks → 20 signups → 2 paid customers ($20 MRR)

## Content Pillars (5 Types)

1. **Hidden Spots** (40%) - "This [place] is X min from [city] but 99% of tourists miss it"
2. **Mistakes & Solutions** (20%) - "5 mistakes I made planning Japan (and how AI fixed them)"
3. **Live Updates** (15%) - "Cherry blossom forecast LIVE update"
4. **Tutorials** (15%) - "How to use Google Maps offline in Japan"
5. **Process Reveals** (10%) - Time-lapse of itinerary optimization

## Production Guidelines

### Visual Style
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (vertical)
- **Resolution:** 1080x1920px minimum
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Color Grading:** Warm, vibrant, Japan-inspired palette
- **Text:** Large, bold, high-contrast (readable on mobile)

### Hook Framework (First 3 Seconds)
1. **Pattern Interrupt:** "Everyone does X... but THIS..."
2. **Shocking Statement:** "I wasted $300 because..."
3. **Curiosity Gap:** "This [place] is [surprising fact]"
4. **Time-Sensitive:** "🌸 LIVE UPDATE:"
5. **Relatable Pain:** "Tokyo subway confusing?"

### CTA Best Practices
- **Keep it simple:** "Link in bio" or "Full guide in bio"
- **Add urgency:** "Limited time" or "Try free"
- **Use emoji:** 🗾 🗺️ ✈️ 🌸 (Japan-themed)
- **Be specific:** "Get offline maps" vs "Learn more"

### Content Sources
- **Stock Footage:** Pexels, Pixabay (Japan travel clips)
- **User-Generated:** Request from community (with permission)
- **Screen Recordings:** App features, map interactions
- **Original:** If budget allows, hire Japan-based creators

### Editing Tools
- **CapCut:** Free, trending effects, easy text overlays
- **InShot:** Mobile editing, music library
- **Adobe Premiere Rush:** Professional quality
- **Canva:** Graphic overlays, text animations

## Risk Mitigation

### Content Risks
- **Issue:** Stock footage looks generic
- **Solution:** Add authentic text overlays, personal insights, app screen recordings

- **Issue:** Running out of hidden spots
- **Solution:** Expand to "Best time to visit X" or "How to avoid crowds at X"

- **Issue:** Audio copyright strikes
- **Solution:** Use Instagram's licensed music library, verify trending sounds

### Growth Risks
- **Issue:** Not hitting view targets
