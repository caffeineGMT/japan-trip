## Success Metrics

### Viral Coefficient Calculation
```
Viral Coefficient = (# of invites sent per user) × (% conversion rate)

Target: 0.3
- Average user sends 3 invites
- 10% convert to paid
- 3 × 0.10 = 0.3 viral coefficient

With 100 users:
→ 100 × 0.3 = 30 new users
→ 30 × 0.3 = 9 new users
→ Total after 2 generations: 139 users (39% growth)
```
