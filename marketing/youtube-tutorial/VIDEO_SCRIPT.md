## 🎥 PRODUCTION NOTES

### Equipment Needed
- **Screen recording:** OBS Studio (free) or ScreenFlow (Mac)
- **Webcam:** 1080p minimum, well-lit
- **Microphone:** USB condenser mic or Rode VideoMic
- **Editing:** DaVinci Resolve (free) or Final Cut Pro

### Filming Tips
1. **Clean desktop:** Hide clutter, close unnecessary apps
2. **Browser zoom:** 125% so text is readable on mobile
3. **Mouse highlights:** Enable yellow circle on click (macOS: System Preferences)
4. **Smooth movements:** No jerky scrolling - slow and deliberate
5. **Test audio:** Record 30 seconds, check for echo/background noise
