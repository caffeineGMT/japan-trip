## 💡 KEY INSIGHTS & DECISIONS

### What Makes This Package Work

**1. Specificity Over Generality**
- Not "how to plan any trip" - specifically "Japan trip in 10 minutes"
- Niche attracts the right audience (high intent travelers)

**2. Promise + Proof**
- Promise: "10 minutes" (bold claim)
- Proof: Live demo showing actual timer
- This builds trust and drives clicks

**3. Free Value First**
- Tutorial shows full workflow (no paywall)
- Builds goodwill before asking for signup
- 10x more likely to convert vs. direct ad

**4. Evergreen + Timely**
- Evergreen: "How to plan" is always relevant
- Timely: Cherry blossom season = annual surge (Feb-April)
- Compounds year over year

**5. Multi-Platform Distribution**
- YouTube (primary)
- Shorts (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube)
- Social (Facebook, Reddit, Twitter)
- Email (personal network)
- Each platform feeds the others

### What Could Go Wrong (And How to Fix)

**Low views (<500 in Week 1):**
- Fix: Promote harder (10 more Facebook groups, 5 more subreddits)
- Fix: Email more people (100 → 500)
- Fix: Create Short version for TikTok/Instagram

**Low CTR (<4%):**
- Fix: A/B test new thumbnail (create 3 variants)
- Fix: Change title (test "This AI Tool" → "Free AI Tool")
- Fix: Add face to thumbnail (increases CTR 30%+)

**Low avg duration (<3:00):**
- Fix: Cut intro to 10 seconds (faster hook)
- Fix: Speed up demo sections (1.2x playback)
- Fix: Add more pattern interrupts (B-roll every 15 sec)

**Low conversion (<5%):**
- Fix: Create dedicated YouTube landing page
- Fix: Reduce signup friction (email only, no password)
- Fix: Add "As Seen in Video" trust signals
