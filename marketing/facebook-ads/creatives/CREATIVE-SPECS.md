## Brand Guidelines

### Typography
- **Primary Font:** Inter (headings)
- **Secondary Font:** Noto Sans JP (Japanese text)
- **Body:** Clean, readable sans-serif

### Colors
- **Primary:** #ef4444 (red - cherry blossom accent)
- **Secondary:** #fce7f3 (light pink)
- **Accent:** #991b1b (dark red)
- **Neutral:** #1f2937 (dark gray)
- **Background:** #ffffff (white)

### Logo
- Use full-color logo on light backgrounds
- White logo on dark/photo backgrounds
- Minimum size: 120px width
- Clear space: 20px all sides

### Photography Style
- High-quality, professional photos
- Natural lighting preferred
- Vibrant but realistic colors
- Avoid heavy filters
- Show people enjoying cherry blossoms when possible

### Emoji Usage
- Use sparingly and strategically
- Cherry blossom 🌸 for main theme
- Maps 🗺️ for navigation features
- AI/robot 🤖 for AI features
- Checkmarks ✅ for feature lists
- Timer ⏰ for urgency

## Production Notes

### Photography Sources
1. **Stock Photos:** Unsplash, Pexels (cherry blossom Japan)
2. **Original Photos:** Hire local photographer in Japan
3. **App Screenshots:** Create in Figma, export high-res

### Video Production
1. **Animation:** After Effects or similar
2. **Map Graphics:** Export from Leaflet/Mapbox
3. **UI Mockups:** Screen recordings + After Effects polish
4. **Captions:** Burned-in during editing

### Quality Standards
- All images professionally edited
- Color correction for consistency
- Remove any watermarks
- Optimize file sizes without quality loss
- Test on mobile devices before launch

## Testing & Optimization

### Image A/B Tests
- Test different cherry blossom locations
- Test with/without people in photos
- Test different color grades (warm vs cool)
- Test text overlay vs no overlay

### Copy A/B Tests
- Different emoji combinations
- Urgency vs value-focused
- Feature list vs benefit-focused
- Short vs long copy

### Performance Benchmarks
- CTR Target: 1.0%+
- CPC Target: $5.00 or less
- Conversion Rate: 10%+ (awareness), 20%+ (retargeting)
- Cost per Signup: $25 or less (retargeting)

## Legal & Compliance

### Disclosures
- "Limited time offer" must have actual expiration
- "50% off" based on regular $9.99/mo price
- All claims must be substantiated

### Ad Policies
- No before/after claims
- No misleading imagery
- Proper attribution for stock photos
- Privacy policy linked on landing page
- Clear pricing disclosure

### Facebook Ad Guidelines
- Text overlay <20% of image
- No prohibited content
- Family-safe imagery
- Accurate representation of product
