## Recording Specifications

### Technical Requirements
- **Format**: MP3
- **Sample Rate**: 44.1 kHz
- **Channels**: Mono (not stereo)
- **Bitrate**: 128 kbps
- **Volume**: Normalized to -3dB
- **Padding**: 1 second silence before and after each phrase
- **Background**: Clean, no noise or echo
- **Equipment**: Professional or prosumer microphone recommended

### Voice Requirements
- **Speaker**: Native Japanese speaker (Tokyo dialect preferred)
- **Gender**: Female voice preferred (more approachable for travel phrases)
- **Age**: 25-40 years old (natural, clear voice)
- **Tone**: Friendly, clear, and slightly slower than conversational speed (for language learners)
- **Consistency**: Same voice, same recording environment for all 44 phrases

## Recording Instructions for Voice Actor

### Setup
1. Find a quiet room with minimal echo (consider using a closet with clothes for sound dampening)
2. Use a quality microphone (USB condenser mic minimum)
3. Position microphone 6-8 inches from mouth
4. Use pop filter to reduce plosives
5. Test recording first 2-3 phrases to check quality

### Recording Process
1. **Before each phrase**: Take a breath, prepare
2. **During**: Speak clearly, naturally, with slight smile in voice
3. **Speed**: Slightly slower than normal conversation (0.9x speed)
4. **After**: Pause for 1 second of silence
5. **Consistency**: Same volume, same distance from mic for all phrases

### Quality Checklist
- ✅ No background noise (AC, traffic, pets, clicks)
- ✅ No mouth sounds or breathing noises
- ✅ Clear pronunciation with natural Japanese accent
- ✅ Consistent volume across all files
- ✅ No clipping or distortion
- ✅ Natural pacing (not rushed, not too slow)
