## 🚀 Key Decisions Made

### Content Format
- **Length:** 15-25 seconds (Instagram sweet spot)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 vertical (mobile-optimized)
- **Hook Timing:** First 3 seconds (pattern interrupt)
- **CTA Placement:** Final 2-3 seconds

### Posting Strategy
- **Frequency:** 1 Reel per day (consistency over volume)
- **Time:** 7pm PT (target audience most active)
- **Hashtags:** 8-10 per post (mix of large, medium, small)
- **Cross-posting:** TikTok + YouTube Shorts (secondary)

### Influencer Approach
- **Tier:** Micro-influencers only (10K-50K followers)
- **Compensation:** Gifted access + commission (not upfront cash)
- **Creative Control:** Full freedom (authentic > scripted)
- **Volume:** 10-15 partnerships (diversified risk)

### Growth Tactics
- **Organic First:** Build proof of concept before spending
- **Reinvest Revenue:** First paid customers fund ad spend
- **Community Focus:** Engagement > broadcasting
- **Data-Driven:** Weekly optimization based on metrics

## 🔧 Risk Mitigation

### Content Risks
- **Issue:** Running out of hidden spots
- **Solution:** Expand to "best time to visit" or "avoid crowds" content

### Growth Risks
- **Issue:** Not hitting view targets
- **Solution:** $5-10/day ad boost on top performers

### Conversion Risks
- **Issue:** Profile visits don't convert to clicks
- **Solution:** A/B test bio optimization, Linktree structure

### Influencer Risks
- **Issue:** Low response rate
- **Solution:** Increase offer to 50% commission, send product first
