## 💡 KEY STRATEGIC DECISIONS

### 1. Target Keyword Selection
**Primary:** "how to plan japan trip" (2,400 searches/month)
- High search volume
- Low competition
- Perfect intent match (people actively planning)

**Secondary:** "japan trip planning tool" (480 searches/month)
- Niche, specific
- High conversion intent
- Less competition

**Long-tail:** "ai japan itinerary planner", "cherry blossom trip planner"
- Very specific
- Easy to rank #1
- High conversion rate

### 2. Video Format: Screen + Webcam
**Why split-screen instead of full-screen recording?**
- Builds trust (viewers see a real person)
- Increases engagement (30%+ higher retention)
- Enables emotion (excitement when features work)
- Professional without being corporate

**Webcam placement:** Bottom-right, 20% size
- Doesn't block important UI elements
- Visible but not distracting
- Industry standard for tech tutorials

### 3. Duration: 8 Minutes
**Why not shorter (5 min) or longer (15 min)?**
- 8 minutes = sweet spot for retention + comprehensiveness
- Covers all features without feeling rushed
- Keeps average view duration above 50% (4 minutes)
- Long enough for 2 mid-roll ads (monetization if eligible)

**Pacing:**
- 20-second hook (grab attention)
- 45-second sections (pattern interrupt every 30-60 sec)
- Live timer (builds credibility)

### 4. Thumbnail: Before/After Split-Screen
**Why this format?**
- Before/after = proven to drive clicks (6-8% CTR)
- Shows transformation (problem → solution)
- High contrast (yellow on white = maximum visibility)
- Readable on mobile (320x180 preview size)

**Text hierarchy:**
- "10 MINUTES" = largest (120pt) - the bold promise
- "AI Trip Planner" = smaller (48pt) - the how
- BEFORE/AFTER labels = context

### 5. Promotion Strategy: Multi-Platform
**Why not just YouTube?**
- YouTube algorithm needs initial signal (views, watch time, likes)
- Personal network = warm audience (higher CTR, engagement)
- Social platforms drive initial 500 views (algorithm picks up from there)
- Reddit/Facebook groups = targeted audience (Japan travelers)

**Promotion timeline:**
- Day 1: All platforms (create momentum)
- Week 1: Daily engagement (reply to comments, share Shorts)
- Week 2-4: Organic growth (algorithm takes over)
